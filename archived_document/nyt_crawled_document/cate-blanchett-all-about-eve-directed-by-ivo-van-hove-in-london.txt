Previous screen-to-stage adaptations have included theater productions of John Cassavetes\u2019s 1977 \u201cOpening Night,\u201d which ran at the Brooklyn Academy of Music in 2008, and Ingmar Bergman\u2019s 1973 mini-series \u201cScenes From a Marriage,\u201d at New York Theater Workshop in 2014.
