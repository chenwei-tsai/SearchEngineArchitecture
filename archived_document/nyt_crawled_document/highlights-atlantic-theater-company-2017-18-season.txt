Many of Mr. McDonagh\u2019s earlier plays, including \u201cThe Beauty Queen of Leenane\u201d and \u201cThe Pillowman,\u201d were produced on Broadway. \u201cHangmen\u201d was nominated for three Oliviers, the equivalent of the Tony Awards, and won for best new play and best set design. The black comedy, about what happens to Britain\u2019s second-best hangman on the day hanging is abolished, is scheduled to run at the Atlantic from Jan. 18 through March 4.
